HOW TO USE THE PETP SCANNER  (the short version)

GET IT (one time):
1. Open the app link in your phone browser.
2. iPhone: Share button -> "Add to Home Screen".
   Android: menu (3 dots) -> "Add to Home screen".
Now it's an app icon. Free forever.

USE IT (every day):
1. Open the app. It scans by itself.
2. Rows with a green star = strong trade ideas. Everything else = wait.
3. Tap a row to see:
      Entry  = where to get in
      Stop   = where to get out if wrong (red)
      Target = where to take profit (green)
      Size   = how much to buy so you only risk 1%
   ...plus the chart and why it scored what it scored.
4. Top buttons:
      Scalp / Day / Swing  = how fast you like to trade
      Consv / Balanced / Aggr = how picky it is (Consv = fewest, best)
5. Tap the bell to get a beep when a new strong setup appears
   (keep the app open for that).

THE ONE RULE:
No green star = no trade. And never risk money you can't afford to lose.
This is information, not financial advice.
