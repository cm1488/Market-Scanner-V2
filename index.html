What do Scalp / Day / Swing and the profiles actually change?
SCALP / DAY / SWING — how fast you trade
These tabs change which candles the scanner reads. Every rule stays identical — but stops, targets and trade duration scale with the candle size.

⚡ Scalp — tap it and:
· Signals are built from 5-minute candles, trend confirmed on 15-minute
· It studies roughly the last 21 hours of price action
· Stops & targets become tight (fractions of a percent) — trades resolve in minutes to a few hours
· Most signals, most noise. Only for when you're actively watching.

☀️ Day — tap it and:
· Signals from 15-minute candles, trend confirmed on 1-hour
· It studies roughly the last 2–3 days
· Stops & targets are medium — trades resolve in hours, usually inside a day
· Best balance of quality vs frequency. The default.

📅 Swing — tap it and:
· Signals from 4-hour candles, trend confirmed on the daily
· It studies roughly the last 6 weeks
· Stops & targets get wide (often several percent) — trades resolve in days to weeks
· Fewest signals, slowest moving. Right if you only check once or twice a day.

CONSV / BALANCED / AGGR — how picky it is
These tabs change the standards a setup must pass. The indicators are the same — the bar to qualify moves.

Conservative — tap it and:
· Only trend-following setups allowed (Pullbacks & Breakouts). Bounce/rejection setups are switched off
· Price must be on the correct side of the 200-bar average (no longs in a downtrend)
· Minimum reward-to-risk 1 : 2 — a setup risking $5 must target ≥$10
· STRONG badge needs score ≥ 70 · tightest RSI band (45–68)
· Result: the fewest signals, the highest quality.

Balanced — tap it and:
· Adds Oversold Bounce & Overbought Rejection setups
· 200-bar average rule removed
· Minimum R:R drops to 1 : 1.5 · STRONG at ≥ 65 · RSI band 40–72
· Result: more signals, slightly lower average quality.

Aggressive — tap it and:
· All setups on, with the loosest triggers (oversold fires at RSI 35 instead of 30, band 38–75)
· Minimum R:R only 1 : 1.2 · STRONG at just ≥ 55
· Result: the most signals — and the most false alarms. Treat each as a suggestion to inspect, not a green light.

What never changes: the indicators, the 0–100 scoring system, and your position sizing (that comes from Account $ and Risk % in Settings).
Starting combos: watching the market → Day + Conservative · checking once a day → Swing + Conservative.

About & data sources
Free & open: market data comes directly from Pacifica's public API (candles, prices, orderbook) with Binance public data as fallback; charts are TradingView's free widget. Nothing is sent anywhere — everything runs on this phone. Alerts (sound/vibration) fire while the app is open with auto-rescan on; phones do not allow free background alerts without a server.
